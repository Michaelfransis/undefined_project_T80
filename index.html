About

Who are we?

We are a full service agency and production house offering a bespoke solution for any client based on budget, creative,  technical requirements and timeline. We're all driven towards the same goal, to create creative projects in the mediums of digital video and still photography.

Our story:

Blow  was founded in 2012 as a creative agency  based in Cairo, specialized in photography and talent representation. With local and international experience in planning and executing shoots in Egypt, Dubai, Beirut and New York,  our in-house team coupled with our network of creative talents, artists and producers expanded collectively as a full agency - production house in 2016 that serves clients from concept to post production.

Our Services

  • Photographic and Video Production.

  • Fashion editorials, films and lookbooks.

  • Online, Social Media asset & content creation.

  • Film library curation.

  • Talent Representation.
